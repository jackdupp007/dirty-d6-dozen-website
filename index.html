Welcome, Fellow Wargamer!

Welcome to **The Dirty d6 Dozen**, your premier destination for tabletop wargaming in the local area! We are a friendly, enthusiastic club dedicated to all things miniature, dice, and epic battles. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or just starting your journey into the vast worlds of Warhammer, X-Wing, Marvel Crisis Protocol, or any other tabletop game, you'll find a welcoming community here.

Our members enjoy a vibrant mix of casual games, competitive play, painting sessions, and narrative campaigns that bring the lore to life. Come roll some dice with us!

About The Dirty d6 Dozen

Founded by a group of passionate hobbyists, The Dirty d6 Dozen strives to be a welcoming and inclusive club for wargamers of all skill levels. We believe in fostering a fun environment where sportsmanship and camaraderie are just as important as tactical prowess.

We meet Every Wednesday at Good Games Greenborough. New members are always welcome to drop by, say hello, and join a game. Don't have an army? No problem! Many of us have spare forces for introductory games.

Our Philosophy

  • Community First: Building friendships and a supportive environment.
  • Learn & Grow: Helping new players get started and veterans refine their skills.
  • Diverse Gaming: Playing a wide array of systems, from fantasy to sci-fi.

Games We Play

While our hearts truly belong to the Warhammer universes (Age of Sigmar & 40,000), our tables see a diverse range of systems:

  • Warhammer Age of Sigmar: Epic fantasy battles in the Mortal Realms.
  • Warhammer 40,000: Grimdark sci-fi warfare in the 41st millennium.
  • Star Wars:Shatterpoint The force powered skirmishes in the Star Wars universe.
  • And many more! If you play it, chances are someone here does too!
