Our Weekly Muster!

What to Expect at The Dirty d6 Dozen Meetups

Thinking of joining us for a game? Fantastic! Our weekly meetups are the heart of the club â€“ a chance to roll dice, connect with fellow hobbyists, and enjoy the worlds of tabletop gaming. Here's what you can expect when you come along:

When & Where

We gather **Every Wednesday** at **Good Games Greenborough**.

The doors are open from 3PM..ish to 10PM.......ish. Feel free to drop in anytime during these hours, though arriving earlier means more game time!

Address: 61 Main St, Greensborough VIC 3088

Plenty of parking available.

What To Bring (And What Not To Worry About)

  • Your Army: If you have one, bring your miniatures ready for battle!
  • Dice & Tools: Your trusty dice, tape measure, and any rulebooks/codices you might need.
  • Enthusiasm: A friendly attitude and a love for the hobby are all you truly need!

Don't have an army yet? No problem! Many club members have spare armies they're happy to lend for an intro game. Just let us know, or drop by to observe and learn. We're happy to teach new players the ropes.

Planning to paint? Bring your current project! We often have members painting and are happy to share tips.

Typical Games Played

While schedules can vary, you'll most often find games of:

  • Warhammer Age of Sigmar
  • Warhammer 40,000
  • Star Wars: Shatterpoint
  • And sometimes other skirmish games or board games!

If you're looking for a specific game, it's always a good idea to [Contact Us / Check Discord] beforehand to see who's playing what.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a cost to attend?

A: Our weekly meetups are free to attend.

Q: Do I need to be a member?

A: There is not offically a member registry, just come and play with and for us and you will be a member.

Q: Can I just come to watch?

A: Absolutely! Feel free to come along, see what games are being played, and chat with members. It's a great way to get a feel for the club.
