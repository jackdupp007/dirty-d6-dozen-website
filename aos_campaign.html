Available Battleplans

Choose your battlefield, Warchief! These are the approved battleplans for the Warband Wars campaign. Each offers unique challenges and opportunities for glory.

Army list should have a 8 wounds or Less hero as your General. Some Battleplans have VP conditions if General is slain.

  • Battleplan: The Ritual (AoS v4) (Narrative Mission)
  • Battleplan: Race to the Relic (500pt Recommended)
  • Battleplan: Spearhead or Custom (Flexible Points)

Remember to review the campaign rules before selecting your mission!
