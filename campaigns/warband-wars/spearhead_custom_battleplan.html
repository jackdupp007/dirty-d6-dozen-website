Battleplan: Spearhead or Custom
For any Age of Sigmar Spearhead or externally sourced Battleplan

How to Use This Battleplan Tracker

This page is designed to help you track scores for any Age of Sigmar game played using a **Spearhead Battleplan** (from the Core Rules or a Spearhead box set) or any **Custom Battleplan** sourced from a General's Handbook, a Narrative Supplement, or a community event.

Simply agree on your chosen battleplan with your opponent, play your game, and use the tracker below to record your Victory Points (VP) and Command Points (CP) as you go. When your game is complete, you can submit the results to update our campaign leaderboard!

Ensure both players have access to and agree upon the rules of the selected battleplan before starting the game.

Your Chosen Battleplan Rules

For this game, refer to the rules for your chosen battleplan:

  • If playing a **Spearhead Battleplan**, consult the Warhammer: Age of Sigmar Core Rules or your Spearhead box set.
  • If playing a **Custom Battleplan**, use the rules provided in your chosen General's Handbook, campaign book, or community document.
  • All Victory Point scoring (Battleplan Objectives, Battle Tactics, Grand Strategies, End-Game VPs) should follow the rules of your chosen battleplan.

This page provides a score tracker, but the detailed rules for your game come from your chosen battleplan source! Keep your battleplan rules handy.
