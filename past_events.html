Our Glorious Past

Archives of Battles Fought & Victories Won

Welcome to the historical archives of The Dirty d6 Dozen! Here we celebrate the epic tournaments, thrilling narrative campaigns, and memorable club gatherings that have shaped our community.

Relive the triumphs, marvel at the painted miniatures, and see who claimed victory in our past engagements. Each event page details the action, showcases top players, and features galleries of the day's action!
